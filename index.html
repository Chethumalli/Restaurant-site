About MANDI STORIES

MANDI STORIES is a popular restaurant in Mangaluru known for its authentic Arabian Mandi and flavorful dishes. Rated 3.7 ★ with 317 reviews, we offer Dine-in, Kerbside Pickup, and Delivery services. Enjoy delicious meals at an affordable price range of ₹200–400 per person.
